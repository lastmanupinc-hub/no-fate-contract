Ecosystem Roadmap

Overview

The No Fate ecosystem follows a structured five-phase development roadmap, progressing from foundational canonical documents through institutional alignment to a long-term vision of global boundary specification as a constitutional layer for deterministic systems.

Each phase builds upon previous work while maintaining the immutability and integrity of canonical documents through cryptographic verification and semantic versioning.

Roadmap Principles

Immutability

Canonical documents remain immutable once published. New versions use semantic versioning (MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH) to extend or revise boundaries without modifying previous releases.

Additive Development

Each phase builds upon previous work without breaking compatibility. Extensions supplement rather than replace earlier specifications.

Community-Driven

Development prioritizes community needs, institutional adoption requirements, and real-world implementation feedback.

Public Good

All canonical documents released under CC BY-ND 4.0 license ensuring free redistribution while preserving integrity.

Cryptographic Verification

Every canonical document accompanied by SHA-256 hash and GPG signature enabling independent verification.

Open Stewardship

Transparent governance model with clear processes for version creation, boundary extension, and stakeholder input.
