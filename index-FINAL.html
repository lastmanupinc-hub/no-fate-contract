What is No Fate?

No Fate is a deterministic boundary framework that distinguishes between questions AI systems can answer mechanically (deterministic outcomes) and questions requiring human judgment (interpretive or discretionary zones).

The framework establishes refusal as correctness: refusing to answer interpretive or discretionary questions is the normatively correct behavior, not a failure.

Core Principle: Humans, not machines, hold final authority over interpretive and discretionary legal questions.

Key Principles

Boundaries, Not Outcomes

No Fate defines boundaries between deterministic and nondeterministic zones. It does not determine legal outcomes.

Refusal as Correctness

Refusing interpretive or discretionary questions is normatively correct behavior, preserving human authority.

Immutable Standards

Canonical documents are immutable and cryptographically verified. Updates use semantic versioning.
